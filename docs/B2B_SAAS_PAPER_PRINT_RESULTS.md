# B2B SaaS Paper Print: Sample Results

This file shows a filled example for the selected use case.

Important note:
- The data in this file and in the tracker is a working benchmark model.
- It is useful for planning, team alignment, and test design.
- It is not live production evidence until you run the same queries on real search engines and AI tools.

## Use Case

- Product theme: B2B SaaS for print procurement and print ordering management
- Working brand used for the lab: PaperPrint Cloud
- Audience: operations teams, procurement teams, office managers, and finance leaders

## What We Filled

- 10 target queries in the raw dataset
- 18 tracker rows covering baseline, optimized AEO, optimized GEO, and comparison results
- Example channels: Google Search, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot

## What We Get From The Current Data

1. AEO is strongest for direct answer queries.
2. GEO is strongest for AI-generated comparison, ROI, security, and evaluation prompts.
3. Pricing and workflow pages improve most when AEO and GEO are combined.
4. Citation-friendly content improves AI mention rate and answer completeness.

## Sample Outcome Summary

- Baseline AEO average visibility score across 4 test rows: 1.75
- Optimized AEO average visibility score across 4 test rows: 4.25
- GEO average visibility score across 6 AI-engine rows: 3.67
- Best combined performance was on workflow and pricing topics where structured answers plus entity-rich context were both present.

## What We Achieve

### 1. Clear content strategy split

- AEO pages should target short, high-intent, question-led search queries.
- GEO pages should target broader buyer prompts where AI engines synthesize multiple signals.

### 2. Better content production priorities

Create these first:
- FAQ definitions
- pricing explanation pages
- ROI guides
- security and compliance pages
- comparison pages with tables

### 3. Stronger measurement discipline

You now have a repeatable system for tracking:
- visibility
- answer quality
- citation presence
- brand mention
- conversion proxy

### 4. Real-world testing roadmap

You can now run the same queries weekly and replace the sample scores with live data.

## Success Criteria For This Project

- AEO success: answer visibility score reaches 4 or higher on core search queries
- GEO success: citation or brand mention appears in 60 percent or more of AI tests
- Business success: click or conversion proxy improves from 1-2 to 4-5 on high-intent pages
