# Experiment 01: AEO FAQ Page

## Goal

Capture answer-style visibility for the query:
- what is print procurement software

## Hypothesis

If the page starts with a direct 40-60 word answer and uses question-led structure, it should perform better in answer engines and snippet-style search results.

## Target Query

- Primary: what is print procurement software
- Secondary: print procurement platform definition
- Secondary: software for managing business print orders

## Draft Page Structure

### H1
What Is Print Procurement Software?

### Direct Answer Block
Print procurement software is a business platform that helps companies order, manage, approve, and track printed materials across teams, vendors, and locations. It reduces manual buying, improves brand consistency, controls costs, and gives procurement teams visibility into print spending and fulfillment workflows.

### Expanded Answer
Companies use print procurement software when print ordering is spread across multiple offices, vendors, or departments. Instead of handling print jobs through email, spreadsheets, or disconnected vendor portals, teams use one central system to request items, route approvals, enforce templates, and monitor delivery status.

### FAQ Sections
## What problems does print procurement software solve?
- inconsistent vendor pricing
- off-brand materials
- manual approval delays
- poor spend visibility
- multi-location ordering issues

## Who uses print procurement software?
- procurement teams
- operations managers
- office administrators
- brand teams
- finance teams

## What features should buyers look for?
- approval workflows
- vendor management
- branded templates
- usage analytics
- budget controls
- delivery tracking
- ERP or procurement integration

## CTA
Book a demo to see how PaperPrint Cloud standardizes print ordering across offices.

## AEO Optimization Checklist

- H2 sections written as direct buyer questions
- first paragraph answers the query without filler
- key answer length kept near 50 words
- terminology kept simple and specific
- one-page focus on a single search intent

## How To Test

1. Publish or stage this page.
2. Search the primary query in Google.
3. Check whether the answer block is visible in search results or People Also Ask.
4. Paste the same query into ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot.
5. Record whether the definition is used, paraphrased, or ignored.

## Success Signal

- Visibility score reaches 4 or higher
- Brand mention appears in at least 2 AI engines
- Answer accuracy is scored 4 or higher
