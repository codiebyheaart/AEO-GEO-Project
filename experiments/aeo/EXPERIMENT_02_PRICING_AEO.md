# Experiment 02: AEO Pricing Page

## Goal

Win high-intent pricing queries for print ordering software.

## Target Query

- how much does print ordering software cost

## Hypothesis

A pricing page that gives a direct cost range, implementation context, and buyer decision factors should outperform a generic sales page for answer-focused queries.

## Draft Answer Block
Print ordering software typically costs between $299 and $2,500 per month depending on user volume, number of locations, approval complexity, vendor integrations, and reporting needs. Enterprise pricing is usually customized when procurement controls, SSO, audit logs, and multi-brand catalog management are required.

## Pricing Explanation Sections

## What affects price?
- number of users
- number of office locations
- workflow complexity
- ERP or procurement integrations
- vendor onboarding requirements
- analytics and compliance controls

## Typical pricing models
- monthly subscription
- annual contract
- usage-based ordering volume
- setup and onboarding fees

## What should enterprise buyers ask?
- Is onboarding included?
- Are approval workflows customizable?
- Is SSO available?
- Can multiple vendors be managed in one dashboard?
- Are audit logs and cost reports included?

## CTA
Request a pricing walkthrough for your print ordering volume and office footprint.

## Test Instructions

1. Publish the pricing page with the answer block at the top.
2. Search the target query and close variants.
3. Check whether search results surface the range or page summary.
4. Ask AI engines for software cost estimates and see whether this page is reflected.
5. Log visibility, completeness, and conversion proxy.

## Success Signal

- Pricing query visibility score improves from 1-2 to 4+
- Conversion proxy improves through demo or pricing clicks
